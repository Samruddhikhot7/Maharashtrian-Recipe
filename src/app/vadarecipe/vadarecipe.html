Vada Pav

Ingredients:

  • 2 Pav buns
  • 2 Batata Vadas (fried potato balls)
  • 1 tbsp Dry garlic chutney
  • Green chili (optional)
  • Butter or oil for toasting

Instructions:

  1. Slice pav buns in half and toast lightly with butter/oil.
  2. Place garlic chutney on one side of the bun.
  3. Add the hot batata vada in between.
  4. Press gently and serve with fried green chili.
